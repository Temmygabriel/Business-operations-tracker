# 📊 Acetrue Technologies — Business Operations Tracker

An Excel-based daily operations and inventory management system 
built for a small electronics retail business.

## Features
- Live inventory tracking across 13 product lines
- Daily sales log with dropdown menus and auto-calculations
- Stock reorder alerts (red/yellow/green status)
- Purchase & restock logging
- Business expense tracking
- Auto-calculated Profit & Loss summary
- Dashboard with KPIs (daily revenue, inventory value, units sold)

## Skills Demonstrated
- Excel formula design (SUMIF, SUMPRODUCT, VLOOKUP, IFERROR)
- Data validation & dropdown lists
- Conditional formatting
- Cross-sheet referencing
- Business process design
